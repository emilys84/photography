Beautiful maternity portraits create a lasting memory of an incredible time in your life. Something to treasure for a lifetime. During your session I will work with you to create portraits that you are proud of and that capture the beauty of your pregnancy.

NEWBORN PHOTOGRAPHY

Posed/studio newborn photoshoot typically must be done within the first 2 weeks of birth when the baby is very sleepy and “mold-able”. The focus in this type of photo session is on shots of the baby looking perfect, usually in blankets, wraps, hats, & headbands. The session can last up to 4 hours with feeding, potty breaks, and posing.
